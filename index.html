The “cosm” in “V-R-Cosm” (V-R-käz-əm) is from the greek root “Kosmos” meaning “world,” as in “microcosms.” “Vrcosm” is a word I made up in order to easily talk about and label a kind of experience in VR that is more than a game or a movie. A vrcosm is a detailed 3d world encapsulized within itself, digitally created to be navigateable in Virtual Reality, and is connectable with other similar VR experiences. A vrcosm can contain games, puzzles, plots, characters, and/or experiences.

This website is still in its infancy, your patience with any glitches is appreciated.

The goal of this website is to investigate themes relating to art, philosophy, psychology, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Mixed Reality, WebVR, Quantum computers, other bleeding edge technologies.

This site is mostly an archive of my musings, rants and my experiments in VR/tech, but my goal for this site is to collect and create many high quality detailed vr experiences and organize them into a coherrent world to be explored by everyone.

Welcome to the Hive Mind

Thoughts about the noosphere, April 17, 2018

In the very nearfuture,there will be a literal labyrinth of virtual worlds each piece intricately designed and ready to be explored. Within the different vr worlds you can play games, you can watch entertainment and “movies” will be acted out by realistic 3d characters in front of you, ready for your participation to decide your adventure. There are great secrets that have yet to be found that are still only known by the ones who created them. As complex and surprising as the real world, This is the frontier of creation is in VR. What VR will be or won't be will not be decided by our technology, but insstead our technology will catch up eventually, what will it look like when it does?

Throughout history there has been a pattern of man conceptualizing impossible things and then against all odds we somehow bring it into existence. From Leonardo's flying machines to the silent film where we send a rocket to the moon. In 1922 a man named Pierre Teilhard de Chardin introduced a new concept, the “noosphere” in his book Cosmogenesis. The greek “nous” means mind, and then “sphere” as in “atmosphere” and “biosphere. The noosphere /ˈnoʊ.əsfɪər/ as I see it, is our next evolutionary step as a species, a connection from one mind to another through technology each brain acting as a kind of neuron inside a greater swarm brain. We've already seen the very first sparks of the noosphere across the internet, nicknamed “the hive mind” usually referred to in a negative sense as the hive being unoriginal and gullible. But is that mob mentality not just the primitive beginnings of our greater mind, the infant hive mind. I have a theory that this group think mentality is only dangerous when it is unchecked where each of the individuals go on a melee. If instead we were to set it up on purpose in a way that would help us rather than harm us.


But it is hardly a WE ARE BORG situation, the noosphere is just what connects us to others. It does not define us as individuals.

You can see seeds of it starting in communities like facebook, reddit, and other social media sites, the bregading and massive group thought leading to sometimes very sorry mistakes.

Thing is we all are nearly identical to one another, that really we could be thought to already of one mind, it is just the space in between that gives us this sense of other peopel being other.

We all have the same universal traits, the same types of thoughts, same emotions, same logic fallacies, the same desires and pleasures, and soon we will all be connected by some kind of technology. That technology (I'm wagering on it being VR/AR) will break down the walls of distance that has kept us isolated from one another. And what wonders we can see in other creatures who swarm, the beautiful areal stunts of birds, and the creativity of large groups of ants. “But how do they all know instantly which way to go?”

They know, because they are no longer just their individual minds, but each is a part of the mind of the whole, each contributing its one decision for the whole. And birds and ants are stupid so stupid! What could a 7 billion strong cloud of humans syncronized into one mind do? One mind, each mind individually contributing to one global decision at a time. Musicians feel it when they play with other musicians, dancers feel it when they dance together. Surrendering to the hive mind.
